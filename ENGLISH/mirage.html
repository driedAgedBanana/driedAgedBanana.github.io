TAKING THE 25TH STEP TOWARDS THE FUTURE

Embark on an enlightening experience as we bring together students from prestigious institutions — SintLucas, Avans University of Applied Science and VU Amsterdam. This event promises to be a captivating exploration into the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in education.

In the inaugural segment, our insightful students will delve into the intricacies of incorporating AI into their current academic endeavors. Their mission? To articulate compelling arguments that persuade the discerning moderator, Samer Mansour, the esteemed CEO, and founder of CEEDS.

The SURF educational day event is not just a culmination; it's a celebration of innovation and forward-thinking. Join us for an immersive experience where the intersection of education and artificial intelligence paves the way for a brighter and more informed future.

Don't miss the opportunity to witness the unfolding of these pioneering ideas and the shaping of the future of education!
