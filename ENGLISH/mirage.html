WHERE THE FOG UNFOLDS

In the heart of Rotterdam, where the city's pulse beats strongest, Floris Huybregts, a young and ambitious police officer, finds himself in a whirlwind of crime, camaraderie, and complex urban narratives. Transplanted from the quiet streets of Valkenswaard, he now navigates the bustling metropolis, where every alley conceals secrets, and every corner holds potential danger. Join Floris as he delves into a world of shadows beneath the glittering surface of the city.

From high-stakes undercover operations to tense standoffs on the cobbled streets, he grapples with the balance between justice and survival. Alongside his enigmatic partner, Levi, and the formidable chief Mandy, they form an unlikely trio, each with their own brand of expertise.

As the sun sets over the Rotterdam harbor, casting long shadows across the skyline, Floris races against time to unravel the threads of a criminal empire that seeks to choke the life out of the city. Amidst the labyrinth of crime, a mysterious force known only as the Six Serpents lurks, casting a dark shadow over Rotterdam's streets.
